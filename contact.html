Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your speech recognition?

Our speech recognition technology achieves up to 95% accuracy for clear audio in supported languages. Factors like audio quality, background noise, accents, and technical terminology can affect accuracy.

Which languages do you support?

We currently support English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified), Russian, and Arabic. We're continuously adding more languages to our platform.

How do I get started with Audiofy?

Simply create a free account on our website, and you'll immediately have access to our basic transcription features. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime to unlock more features and transcription minutes.

Is my data secure with Audiofy?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All audio and transcription data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
